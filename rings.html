Spoon Rings

Each of these rings is handmade by me using a hammer and mandrel. I recently upgraded to a ring press, which makes the process a bit easier on my hands — but every piece is still crafted with care. all of the spoons are sourced from local thrift shops and yard sales, and over time I’ve built up quite the collection. If you're interested in getting one of your own, feel free to reach out! I'm always happy to take custom requests.
