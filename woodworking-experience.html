Woodworking Background

I’ve been woodworking since 2015, starting off with set design for school plays in my middle schol. I continued this until college and have since taught myself all sorts of different carpentry and woodworking skills. Now I have built everything from handcrafted pens to custom furniture pieces. While not a traditional job, my hands-on experience has given me expertise in joinery, finishing, and working with exotic hardwoods. Every project sharpens both my craft and creativity.
